\par Let $M_m$ be a measurement operator. Then $E_m = M_m^* M_m$ is Hermitian
and will thus have a spectral decomposition $U \Lambda U^*$ with unitary $U$. We
contend that $U$ satisfies $M_m = U \sqrt{E_m}$:

\begin{align}
M_m^* M_m &= \left( U \sqrt{E_m} \right)^* U \sqrt{E_m} \\
&= \left( \sqrt{E_m} \right)^* U^* U \sqrt{E_m} \\
&= \sqrt{E_m^*} \sqrt{E_m} \\
&= \sqrt{E_m} \sqrt{E_m} = E_m
\end{align}
