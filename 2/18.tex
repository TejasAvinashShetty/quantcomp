\par Let $U^* = U^{-1}$ and let $\ket{x}$ be a normalized eigenvector of $U$
with eigenvalue $\lambda_x$. Then $\abs{\lambda_x}^2 = \lambda_x^* \lambda_x
\braket{x|x} = \bra{x} \lambda_x^* \lambda_x \ket{x} = \left( \bra{x|U^*}
\right) \left( \ket{U|x} \right) = \braket{x|U^{-1} U|x} = \braket{x|x} = 1$.
