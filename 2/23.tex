\par Let $P$ be a projector and let $P \ket{x} = \lambda \ket{x}$. Then $P
\ket{x} = P^2 \ket{x} = P \lambda \ket{x} = \lambda P \ket{x} = \lambda^2
\ket{x}$, thus $\lambda^2 - \lambda = \lambda \left( \lambda - 1 \right) = 0$.
